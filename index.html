♥ ABOUT US ♥

JULIE
VERA

the bride

Robby and I met in the summer of 2008. I was an almost-senior at Northeastern and president of a student organization that required I make a presentation to incoming transfer students. Since Robby's major didn't exactly line up with my audience, I completely ignored his side of the room! Of course, after the presentation was over, only one student came up to the front to ask questions.

I blew off the next couple of meetings I had, assuring him that they "weren't important" (they were). We talked for hours over a tray of Oreos and I inadvertently made him late for his adviser appointment.

Why I'm Marrying Robby...
  • 1. He knows how to cook. And not just eggs and toast.

  • 2. He has the prettiest cornflower blue eyes (and his beard is red, which is an awesome and hilarious combination)!

  • 3. He is the perfect combination of silly, sweet, and smaahhht (that's how we say SMART in Boston).

  • 4. He understands my special brand of weirdness.

  • 5. He never fails to present me with crab rangoons at the most opportune moments.

ROBBY GRODIN

the groom

In the Spring of 2008, before transferring to Northeastern, I attended an academic club conference on Long Island. I immediately noticed the students from Northeastern. One girl stood out in particular: purple hair, punky jewelry, a union Jack vest, and plaid pants that I mistook for pajamas. The cutest girl in the room. And through the whole weekend we never crossed paths. In fact, I wouldn't see her again until Summer 2008...

I met Julie at my transfer orientation and was instantly smitten. A few weeks later I lent her my hoodie, we built a fort out of boxes and sheets, and the rest is your atypical non-standard Boston love story.

Why I'm Marrying Julie...
  • 1. We share EVERYTHING. Meals, drinks, clothes and colds.

  • 2. Whenever Julie is within reach, delicious cupcakes are NEVER far way.

  • 3. She smells really, really nice. No seriously, see for yourself.

  • 4. She fits in my backpack so I can take her anywhere!

  • 5. When you've found a truly exceptional and beautiful woman like Julie, you don't let go. Ever.
